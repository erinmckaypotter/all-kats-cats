All Kat's Cats

Cheeks the cat is a pretty little princess, and expects to be treated as such. Her favorite activities include sleeping, eating, and having her ample and luscious belly rubbed.

Occasionally she goes for a stroll, or a brief nap, in the garden...but only sometimes. For the most part, she's quite content to live the rest of her life in her human's (exuse me, HER) bed.
