Clementine

about this lil' baby

Clementine is really old. Like REALLY old. Like we're not really sure how old she is, but she was already living there when they moved in like 15 years ago. And her meow sounds like she's been smoking a pack of cigarettes a day for every single one of those years.

No, wait...scratch cigarettes - make it cigars. She sounds like she's been smoking big ol' cigars everyday for the last 15 years. Everyone told her you're not supposed to inhale, but she did it anyway. And now her voice sounds like a tortured ghost...or like blender that's struggling to turn because its got too much gunk in the blades.

In spite of her scary voice, Clementine (as the title of this page would suggest) is a sweet lil' baby. She's super cuddly and loves getting pets almost as much as she loves food. You have to be careful not to step on her at night though, because she lays in the middle of the floor, and she's black, which renders her nearly invisible to the naked eye in dim lighting.
