Cheeks

about this lil' puddin' ball

Cheeks is a lil' dollop, a lil' puddin' ball, a lil' muffin, a lil' loaf of cuteness. Her favorite activities include sleeping, eating, and having her ample and luscious belly rubbed. Occasionally she goes for a stroll, or a brief nap, in the garden... but only sometimes. For the most part, she's quite content to live the rest of her life in her human's (exuse me, HER) bed.

Cheeks used to be Kat's cat, but now she lives with me. She was scared of all the other cats, because she's kind of wierd and agoraphobic. The other cats were too wordly and tough, and they picked on her. She loves her new life being the only cat in her house.

Surprising as it is looking at that luscious belly, Cheeks religiously eats only kitty kibble. She has no interest in human food. Even more surprising though, I realized recently that she was somehow maintaining her billowing blob-like figure AND A TAPEWORM at the same time. Don't worry now, the tapeworm is long gone, we got rid of that. However, it did do a real number on her diet. In spite of her strict rations, she's only gotten squishier since we got rid of the tapeworm.
