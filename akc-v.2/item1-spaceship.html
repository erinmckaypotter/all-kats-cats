Spaceship

about this lil' mountain lion

Spaceship is a lil' mountain lion. He's bigger than all the other cats and has those little tufty things on his ears that wild cats have. Once he disappeared for a whole year (presummably because he didn't like human Kat's noisy grandchildren) and returned chubby and totally unscathed. I'm convinced he was out there whoopin' mountain lion ass.

These days though, he's pretty much a lazy people cat. He spends his days asleep on the kitchen table until someone gives him the boot. Then he sits on the armchair and swats at people as they walk by. That sounds aggressive, but he's just trying to hustle pets. He's by far the most agressively social cat I've ever met.

Obviously he eats cat food, like a normal cat, but let's be honest...Spaceship's a spoiled asshole, and kitty kibble is not his favorite. He prefers whatever your eating, or whatever you left on the counter last night. However, he is also pretty fond of hamburger and canned tuna.
